About me

Hello, I‘m psychologist-psychotherapist Giedrė Dovydaitytė-Vaisova. I‘m offering individual psychological counseling for adults in Vilnius city.

Education and work experience:

  • 2018 - 2021 postgraduate studies in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (Lithuanian Health Sciences University).
  • 2018 EMDR Europe specialist certificate.
  • 2017 - now I am working as a medical psychologist in Antakalnis polyclinic.
  • 2017 Master of Clinical Psychology (Vilnius University).
  • 2016-2017 Consultant at “Pagalba pokalbiu”.
  • 2015 Bachelor of Psychology (Vilnius University).
  • 2014-2017 Volunteer at youth hot-line “Jaunimo linija”.
  • I am member of Lithuanian psychologists‘ association, EMDR Lithuania association and Lithuania trauma psychology association.
